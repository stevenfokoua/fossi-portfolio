    Process Dossier

    How the documents map into the portfolio story.

    Each PDF contributes a different layer of evidence. Rather than uploading them as an unstructured archive, this portfolio uses them to show progression from requirement definition to concepting, interface design, system architecture, and final communication.

    01 / Problem Framing

    Topic Description

    Establishes the client context, the force-verification need, the target users, and the operating range. In the portfolio, this becomes the opening challenge statement and constraints block.

    02 / Concept Exploration

    Overall Product Concept

    Shows enclosure requirements, robustness concerns, rechargeability, data interfaces, and a morphological box. This supports a section about design trade-offs and early product architecture.

    03 / Usability Layer

    HMI Final

    Defines glove-friendly interaction, high-contrast status visibility, logging behavior, and calibration workflow. This becomes a dedicated interface-thinking subsection within the case study.

    04 / Technical Depth

    Group Project Report

    Provides the strongest systems view: six load cells, automatic detection, 24-bit ADC, STM32-class MCU, touch HMI, data export, and audit trail requirements. This anchors the engineering detail section.

    05 / Market Narrative

    Brochure + Manual

    Recasts the project as a product with benefits, compatibility, runtime, safety, and portability. In the portfolio, this becomes the polished outcome and communication layer.

    06 / Industry Grounding

    Internship Report

    Adds early practical exposure to automation products, manufacturing, quality, and engineering models. It strengthens the experience narrative and shows industrial context beyond coursework.
